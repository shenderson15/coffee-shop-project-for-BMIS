Our Story

Founded by Sophie Henderson as part of her Hogan Entrepreneurial Leadership Program project at Gonzaga University, The Study Spot was created with a vision to offer a welcoming space for students and the Spokane community. What started as a simple coffee shop has now grown into a favorite destination for Gonzaga, Whitworth, Eastern, SCC, and WSU students, as well as locals who enjoy artisan coffee and delicious snacks.

Our Mission

At The Study Spot, we strive to create a warm and inviting atmosphere where everyone feels welcome. Our mission is to serve high-quality coffee brewed from the finest beans sourced from sustainable farms, paired with a variety of gluten-free and vegan snacks. We want our shop to be a place for students to study, friends to gather, and individuals to unwind over a delicious cup of coffee.

Community & Live Music

Not just a coffee shop, The Study Spot is a community hub. We proudly support local artists by hosting live music every Thursday, giving customers a chance to relax and enjoy live performances while sipping on their favorite drink. Whether you're here for the music or simply to enjoy a quiet moment, we welcome everyone with open arms.
