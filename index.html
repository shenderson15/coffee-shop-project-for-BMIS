From Gonzaga Grad to Spokane Favorite About Our Coffee Shop

Founded by a proud Gonzaga graduate as part of her Hogan Entrepreneurial Leadership Program project, our coffee shop has become a beloved gathering spot for students and the Spokane community alike. Whether you're a student from Gonzaga, Whitworth, Eastern, SCC, or WSU, or a local Spokane resident, our cozy shop welcomes you with open arms.

At our coffee shop, we take pride in offering rich, hand-crafted coffee brewed from the finest beans. But we're not just about great coffee—we also offer a variety of gluten-free and vegan snacks, ensuring that everyone can enjoy a delicious treat with their drink. Our goal is to make our space welcoming for all, whether you're grabbing a quick cup to go or sitting down to study or hang out with friends.

And if you're in town on a Thursday, don't miss our live music sessions, where local artists bring their talent to our warm, inviting space. It's the perfect way to unwind and enjoy a cup of coffee while listening to great tunes!

Join us for your daily routine, a study break, or simply to relax and enjoy the vibrant atmosphere that makes our coffee shop a unique spot in Spokane. We can’t wait to welcome you!
